March 20, 2026 — Edward Tufte, Envisioning Information
"To clarify add detail. Clutter and confusion are failures of design, not attributes of information."
My Thought: This suggests that "minimalism" isn't about removing data, but organizing it so the eye knows where to rest. It’s like an icon system where the weight of the lines dictates the hierarchy of the action.
